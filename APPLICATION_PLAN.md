## LeadGen AI Web App

* Lead Discovery: Search for potential business partners by filtering through specific industries and geographic locations.
* Company Enrichment: View a detailed profile of a business including its background and size before reaching out.
* Personalized Messaging: Create high-quality introductory emails using smart automated suggestions tailored to each prospect.
* Quality Scoring: See a simple numerical rank for every lead to help you focus on the most promising opportunities.
* Activity Dashboard: Keep track of which companies you have already contacted using a clear and organized list view.
* System Configuration: Manage account profiles, AI intelligence models, and notification preferences in a central hub.
